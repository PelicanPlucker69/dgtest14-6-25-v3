ABOUT OUR CLINIC

Philosophy

See more ▼

At Dental Garden, we are committed to creating a serene and welcoming environment where every visit feels calm and stress-free. Our philosophy blends gentle, comprehensive care with clinical excellence and eco-friendly practices. With a strong focus on integrity and evidence-based dentistry, we strive to deliver the highest standard of treatment. Welcome to Dental Garden, where your comfort and smile come first.

Community

See more ▼

At Dental Garden, we’re passionate about giving back to the community. Led by Dr Frances Ershad, our team supports Filling the Gap by volunteering pro bono dental care to those in need. We also proudly contribute to local causes, including Boronia Park Public School and the Gladesville Ravens Women’s Football Team. Through these efforts, we aim to promote education, healthy living, and meaningful connections—because creating smiles goes beyond dentistry.

Technology

See more ▼

At Dental Garden, we use cutting-edge technology to improve diagnosis, treatment, and comfort. From 3D imaging and low-radiation digital X-rays to same-day CEREC crowns, our tools ensure efficient, high-quality care. Ceiling-mounted TVs add a touch of relaxation—because your comfort matters as much as your smile.

Children

See more ▼

At Dental Garden, we make dental visits fun and comforting for kids. With ceiling TVs, take-home toys, treatment flavour options, and sensory tools for anxious children, we create a child-friendly space. Early hours and weekend appointments help fit your schedule. Our goal is to deliver great care and lasting positive memories.

Seniors

See more ▼

At Dental Garden, we care for the unique dental needs of seniors with compassion and expertise. Seniors and pensioners receive 10% off all services, including gentle extractions, tooth replacement options like dentures, bridges, and implants, and full gum care. We’re here to support healthy smiles at every age in a warm, welcoming space.
