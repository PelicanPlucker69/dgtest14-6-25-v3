The Dental Garden Story

Welcome to Dental Garden, where exceptional dental care thrives in a serene, garden-inspired setting. I'm Dr. Frances Ershad, owner and principal dentist, and I'm thrilled to share our journey with you.

At Dental Garden, we believe in treating our patients like family. Inspired by the harmonious blend of art and science, I created a space where dental care and tranquility coexist. Our lush, green environment provides a calming atmosphere from the moment you arrive.

My journey to dentistry was unconventional yet profoundly transformative. From an early age, I was fascinated by science and biology, always eager to learn about the natural world. My other great passion was visual art—if I wasn’t drawing or painting, I was out exploring. This love for both creativity and discovery eventually shaped my path.

While studying for a degree in Environmental Science, I discovered my passion for dental care while working as a dental assistant. Encouraged by my mentor, I pursued a Doctor of Dental Medicine at the University of Sydney, driven by a desire to offer high-quality care with genuine compassion.

Outside of the clinic, I enjoy spending time with my Jack Russell, Milo, whether it’s running in the park, bushwalking, or tending to my garden. I'm also dedicated to community service, volunteering with Filling the Gap, the Australian Dental Health Foundation, and at ADA clinics for dental rescue days.

Dental Garden is a reflection of my commitment to excellence. Our state-of-the-art equipment and top-tier materials ensure you receive the highest standard of care. Our team specializes in a wide range of services, from gentle cleanings to advanced cosmetic, orthodontic, and implant procedures, all under one roof.

Here at Dental Garden, you’re more than a patient—you’re part of our family. Let’s grow together, creating beautiful smiles in a tranquil setting.
