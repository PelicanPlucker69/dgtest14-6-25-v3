Treatment Disclaimer

At Dental Garden, we are committed to providing exceptional dental care. However, it is essential to understand the following:

1. Individual Results May Vary: Dental treatments can yield different outcomes for each patient. Factors such as oral health, genetics, and lifestyle play a role in treatment effectiveness.

2. Consultation Required: Before undergoing any dental procedure, we recommend scheduling a consultation with our experienced clinicians. They will assess your unique needs and recommend personalised treatment options.

3. Informed Consent: Prior to any treatment, we ensure that patients receive comprehensive information about the procedure, potential risks, benefits, and alternatives. Informed consent is crucial for your peace of mind.

4. No Guarantees: While we strive for optimal results, we cannot guarantee specific outcomes. Dental treatments are influenced by various factors, and individual responses vary.

5. Continual Care: Dental health requires ongoing maintenance. Regular check-ups, hygiene practices, and adherence to post-treatment instructions contribute to long-lasting results.

Remember that our team is here to guide you through your dental journey. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to us.

Note: This disclaimer serves as general information and does not replace professional advice. Always consult our dental experts for more guidance.
